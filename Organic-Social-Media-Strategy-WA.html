Weather Academy

Organic Social Media Strategy

Instagram: @weather_academy

Led the end-to-end organic social media strategy for Weather Academy, a European weather education platform focused on making complex meteorological data accessible and engaging for everyday audiences. Acted as the bridge between scientific credibility and audience relatability — translating dense atmospheric data into structured, visual-first content systems that drive consistent organic growth, sustained engagement, and long-term brand authority across Instagram.

This project centers on building a strong organic social media presence by transforming complex atmospheric data into structured, engaging, and relatable content. The strategy focuses on developing a scalable content system, defining a clear brand positioning, and creating consistent storytelling that balances educational depth with everyday relevance. By aligning content with audience needs and behavior, the project aims to drive sustainable engagement, strengthen brand authority, and convert high-performing content into long-term audience growth.

Despite having strong niche credibility in weather and science education, Weather Academy faced challenges in translating this potential into consistent and scalable growth. The content was informative, but lacked a clear structure, positioning, and system that could sustain long-term audience engagement.

  • Lack of a clear content structure, resulting in inconsistent posting and growth
  • Brand positioning was not clearly defined across content
  • Absence of structured content pillars and repeatable formats
  • Limited relatable, everyday content to engage a broader audience

To address these challenges, the strategy focused on building a strong foundation through clear positioning, structured content systems, and scalable formats. The goal was to transform content from being reactive and inconsistent into a cohesive, repeatable, and growth-driven ecosystem.

  • Defined a clear brand positioning to establish authority and consistency
  • Developed structured content pillars to guide content direction
  • Introduced repeatable content formats for scalability
  • Leveraged short-form and visual-first content to drive engagement
  • Balanced educational content with relatable, real-life context
  • Built a consistent content system to support long-term organic growth
