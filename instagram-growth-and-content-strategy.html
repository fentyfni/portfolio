Instagram Growth & Content Strategy

Instagram: @heejuptogo___

Heejuptogo is a local fashion brand focusing on modest wear with a youthful and trend-driven aesthetic. The brand targets Gen Z and student audiences through digital platforms, with Instagram as its primary channel for brand discovery and engagement.

Spearheaded a 3-month digital transformation for Heejuptogo, a local fashion brand. The project focused on reshaping the brand's digital presence by integrating organic content strategy with high-efficiency paid advertisements to capture a wider and more active audience.

Heejuptogo faced challenges in expanding its digital reach and activating audience engagement despite having an existing Instagram presence.

  • Low organic reach and limited audience growth.
  • Inconsistent content performance and engagement.
  • Instagram profile and bio not yet optimized to clearly communicate brand value.
  • Paid ads had not been maximized to support organic content performance.

I led a 3-month Instagram growth and content optimization strategy by integrating organic content development with performance-driven paid advertisements.

Key Initiatives:

  • Content Strategy Revamp

    Developed a structured content framework focusing on:

    • Product storytelling
    • Educational & relatable fashion content
    • Trend-adaptive reels and visual formats
    • Clear CTA to encourage saves, shares, and profile visits

  • Instagram Profile Optimization

    • Optimized Instagram bio to clearly communicate brand positioning, value proposition, and shopping intent
    • Improved profile structure to support discoverability and conversion

  • Organic + Paid Synergy

    • Identified top-performing organic content and scaled them through paid ads
    • Used paid ads not only for reach, but to boost engagement quality (saves, shares, interactions)
    • Applied efficient audience targeting to reach relevant fashion & modest wear audiences

  • Performance Monitoring & Iteration

    • Continuously analyzed insights (reach, engagement rate, interaction source)
    • Adjusted creatives, copy, and ad formats to maximize understanding and recall

Key Features

  • Strategic Content Framework
  • Audience & Insight-Driven Planning
  • Visual & Brand Repositioning
  • Organic + Paid Growth Strategy
  • Performance Tracking & Optimization
  • Scalable Content System
