Campaign & Talent Collaboration Pitch

Instagram: @godslave.ent

GodSlaves is a talent agency representing digital creators with strong personal personas and niche audiences. The agency focuses on building authentic brand collaborations by aligning creator identity, audience behavior, and brand objectives into culturally relevant and platform-native campaigns.

Developed a strategic pitch deck for a potential collaboration between Glad2Glow, a skincare brand, and Putri Desebel, a chaotic beauty content creator, under GodSlaves talent agency. The project focused on translating brand objectives and creator persona into a high-engagement Reels and TikTok campaign concept tailored to Gen Z audiences.

As a talent agency, GodSlaves needed to present collaborations that felt organic and credible to brands, while staying true to the creator’s established persona and audience expectations.

  • Ensuring strong alignment between brand values and creator identity.
  • Avoiding overly promotional content that could weaken creator authenticity.
  • Convincing brand stakeholders of creator–brand fit through strategic storytelling.

To address these challenges, I developed a strategic collaboration concept that balanced brand credibility with creator authenticity. The pitch emphasized emotional storytelling, humor, and platform-native formats tailored to Gen Z behavior on Reels and TikTok.

  • Built a clear campaign narrative connecting brand goals and creator persona.
  • Defined content pillars aligned with audience behavior and platform trends.
  • Structured the pitch deck to highlight creator fit, engagement logic, and storytelling flow.
