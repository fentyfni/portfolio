Rally & Relate 2025

Instagram: @kgtennisacademy

KGTennis Academy is Kompas Gramedia’s official sports community, positioned at the intersection of lifestyle, networking, and corporate engagement. Operating within one of Indonesia’s largest media groups, the brand leverages sports as a platform for relationship-building and brand collaboration.

Developed a premium sports and networking event concept, Rally & Relate 2025, under KGTennis Academy to connect professionals, executives, and business owners through tennis and padel. The project focused on uniting sportsmanship and business synergy into a cohesive, sponsor-ready event narrative within Kompas Gramedia’s ecosystem.

As a corporate sports community, KGTennis Academy needed a strong narrative to position its event as more than just a sports gathering, while remaining attractive to sponsors and partners.

  • Positioning sports as a credible business networking platform.
  • Creating a compelling, sponsor-ready event narrative.
  • Aligning lifestyle storytelling with corporate communication standards.

To address these challenges, I developed a comprehensive event narrative and sponsorship communication strategy that unified sportsmanship, business synergy, and lifestyle engagement under one cohesive identity.

  • Built a clear event concept and storytelling structure.
  • Defined partnership value and sponsorship messaging.
  • Aligned visual tone and communication style with Kompas Gramedia’s brand ecosystem.
  • Mapped media exposure to support visibility and partner value.

Key Features

  • Integrated Event Storytelling
  • Sponsorship & Partnership Framework
  • Audience-Centric Concept Design
  • Media Ecosystem Activation
  • Visual & Narrative Direction
  • Community & Brand Synergy Strategy
